Our Story

Individualism: The principle of individualism is to have the confidence to be the main character in your story. A book symbolizes individualism because books can relate to an individuals life in many ways.
Each chapter you reach in the book takes you farther to the end. At the end, you are more familiar with the characters and the plot. Just as each year takes to another chapter in your life--closer to the end.
By the end of your life, you are familiar with who you are as an individual.
